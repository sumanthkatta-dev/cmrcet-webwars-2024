Admissions

How to get in (2024 intake).

Entrance based. Keep your rank card handy. We keep the process simple and transparent.

Eligibility

B.Tech: EAMCET. M.Tech: GATE/PGECET. MBA/MCA: ICET. Lateral entry via ECET.

Application

Share rank, marks, and branch preference. Counseling help is on campus and on call.

Scholarships

Merit scholarships for good ranks. Limited fee support for deserving students.

Onboarding

Short bridge courses, quick coding bootcamps, English labs, and mentor allocation.
